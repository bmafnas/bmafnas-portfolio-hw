BRIANNA MAFNAS (bree-ann-uh)(moff-ness) was born and raised in Guam and is half Chamorro (Guam native) and half Filipino. She moved to Southern California in 2002 and lived in the San Francisco Bay Area for several years before returning to the Greater Los Angeles Area in 2010. Brianna received her B.A. in Art: Graphic Design/Visual Communication from Cal State LA and is currently an in-house Senior Designer for a toy and game manufacturing company. She specializes in brand identity, packaging design and print with a working knowledge in UX/UI design.
